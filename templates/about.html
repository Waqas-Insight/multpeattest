About Us

This is the home of the Peatland Policy Portal, a collaborative effort of the EU Life MultiPeat project.

The purpose of this public toolkit is to showcase and analyse peatlands, projects, and policies in order to aid the conservation of peatlands and the management of agriculture on peat soils.

Let's save the peatlands together!
